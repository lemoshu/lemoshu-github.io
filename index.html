Biography [CV]

I am currently a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Raymond Tong, supported by Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). I got my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng. Before that, I finished my master in School of Biomedical Engineering, Health Science Center, Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Dong Ni. I finished my undergraduate with top rank and National Scholarship in the Department of Biomedical Engineering, South-Central University for Nationalities. During my master period, I was offered the chance to visit the IDEA Lab, UNC-Chapel Hill, and supervised by Prof. Dinggang Shen. During my PhD period, I got the ORAP Scholarship and got a visiting chance in Cardiac Magnetic Resonance Center, Harvard Medical School.

My research interest includes medical image computing, intelligent ultrasound, cardiac image analysis and computer graphics.
